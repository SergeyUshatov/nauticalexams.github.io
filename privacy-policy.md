## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect are:

### a) Information You Provide to Us
* **Account Information:** When you create an account using **Google Sign-In**, we collect your name and email address as provided by Google. We use this information solely for authentication and to identify you within the App.

### b) Information We Collect Automatically
* **Usage Data:** We use analytics services (**Google Analytics for Firebase**, **Google Analytics**) to automatically collect information about your interactions with the App. This includes:
    * Screens visited.
    * Features used.
    * Session duration.  
    * *Purpose:* This data helps us understand user behavior and improve the App.
* **Crash Data:** We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect diagnostic data when the App crashes. This includes device information (model, OS version) and the state of the App at the time of the crash to help us diagnose and fix bugs.
* **Advertising ID:** We use **Google AdMob** to serve advertisements. If you consent to personalized advertising, your device's Advertising ID may be used by Google to show relevant ads. You can manage this consent in Section 5.
* **Purchase Information:** If you subscribe to our services, we collect purchase history through **Google Play Billing** to manage your subscription and provide access to premium features.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1.  **To Provide and Maintain the App:** To authenticate users, process subscriptions, and ensure core functionality.
2.  **To Improve Our App:** To analyze usage patterns and crash reports to enhance user experience and fix bugs.
3.  **To Provide Advertising:** To display personalized or non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob, supporting the App's development.
4.  **Future Communications:** We may use your email address to send important updates about the App or respond to support requests. **We will not send marketing communications without your explicit consent.**

## 4. How We Share Your Information

**We do not sell your personal data.** We share information with third-party service providers only as described below:

| Provider Type | Service Used | Data Shared |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cloud Services** | Google Firebase (Firestore) | Account data (Name, Email) |
| **Authentication** | Google Sign-In | Identity credentials |
| **Analytics** | Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics | Usage patterns, crash logs, device info |
| **Advertising** | Google AdMob | Advertising ID |
| **Payments** | Google Play Billing | Subscription/Purchase history |

*Note: All subscription payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not handle or store your payment card information.*

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

You have control over your information through the following methods:

*   **Account Deletion:** You can delete your account at any time from within the App. Upon deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within **30 days**.
*   **Personalized Advertising:** You can manage consent via the consent management form shown upon the first launch of the App. Alternatively, you can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out via your device settings.
*   **Accessing Your Data:** Since we only store information provided by Google, this data is accessible to you through your **Google Account**. We do not provide a separate profile editing screen within the App.

## 6. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information and implement reasonable security measures, including:
*   Using secure protocols (**SSL/TLS**) for data transmission via Firebase.
*   Restricting access to personal data to authorized personnel only.
*   **No Password Storage:** Authentication is managed entirely by Google Sign-In; we do not store user passwords on our servers.
